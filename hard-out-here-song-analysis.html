Hard Out Here - Song Analysis

Like most humans, I enjoy listening to music. It is definitely something I do on a daily basis and can't live without, and which helps me in my creative process at work, among other things. The following will cover my take on the astounding song Hard Out Here by Raye whose message aligns quite well with my feminist values.
The song can be summarized as follows: a woman ranting about the struggles she endured in her professional journey due to the system of 'male privilege' we live in and the toll this took on her mental health.
To delve into the exact lyrics of the song:
-Mental health issues are illustrated by references to faking happiness; addiction problems; and seeking mental clarity through spiritual practice.
-Male privilege is bluntly stated in sentences such as 'what you know about systems?' and 'all the white men CEOs, fuck your privilege'.
-Success achievement struggles are portrayed in content describing how the woman had to hustle her way out; how she was often silenced and deceived by influential men; how her experience felt like she has lived through hell; and how she often 'had to bounce back' from adversities.
I expect this song to be relatable to many women out there who often can't even voice such concerns openly and/or publicly.
To give context into Raye's specific experience:
Raye is primarily talking about her experience in the music industry as a singer and songwriter (notice the use 'my pen is a gun'). She had been signed to a record label who promised her the release of four albums, but she struggled deeply throughout the years while her label failed to deliver on even a single album release. She battled with addiction and she's a Christian believer who often found refuge in God. After she eventually decided to ditch her label and released an album independently, she achieved the fame she was looking for.
Her album called ‘My 21st Century Blues’ is quite nice to hear. The artistry in the lyrics composition and the songs' delivery is immense, and I suggest you check it out.
Some other album teasers: RAYE, 070 Shake - Escapism and RAYE - Oscar Winning Tears.
